About Me

My name is Jasmine Epps. I am a Foreign-Service Brat who spent the first 18 years of life traveling around the world. I attended various international schools abroad, spending my secondary school years in Istanbul Turkey. I received my B.A. in International Relations from Tufts University in New England, and spent time studying abroad at the University of Cape Town, South Africa-with time leading up to the 2010 World Cup. I enjoy learning about other cultures and hope to someday settle somewhere permanently abroad.
